ProAct: Masks

Light, wearable protection

Masks?

Face masks are one of the most recognizable and memorable measures put in place to counteract the virus. These masks are required in public by many countries around the world, including the Philippines. Our government has placed many guidelines and protocols when it comes to these masks. These guidelines also include where, when, and how these masks should be worn. Additionally, the Philippine government has also required the use of face shields on top of masks.

In this website, you'll learn about the different types of masks that you can wear to protect yourself against the COVID-19 virus. You'll learn the strong and weak points of some of the most popular types of masks so that you can make an informed decision about what will protect you the best. This website also includes useful tips from the WHO and CDC involving masks. Continue reading to learn more about different types of masks!

Types of Masks

To the left, you can see an image of a surgical mask. These are one of the most common masks worn by people, especially in the Philippines. They provide sufficient protection if worn properly. Protection while wearing these masks can be improved if the wearer wears more than one mask and wears many layers of protection. Additionally, these masks are one of the preferred masks of surgeons and medical professionals.

Moving on, we see pictures of cloth masks. These masks can protect you from COVID 19, but there are some consideration that you need to make when choosing to wear a cloth mask. These considerations include:

  1. Two layers of fabric are recommended for protection
  2. Making sure that the mask is snug on your face
  3. Make sure to have a suitable fabric that does not make it hard to breathe.
  4. Masks that have vents and holes in them should not be worn
You can watch the video below to learn more!

Moving on, we now look at N95 masks. These types of masks provide great protection. However, these are not recommended for use by regular civilians. According to the CDC, these masks should be prioritized for the use of medical professionals, especially if the mask is labelled 'surgical N95'.

According to the CDC, face shields alone are not recommended as a replacement for masks They have very large gaps and let a lot of outside air in. This is to be expected, as they are primarily meant for protection of the eyes and face. However, they still have their uses during this pandemic. They can especially be used as an addition to masks or for people who have medical issues that make wearing masks hard.

Summary

Now that you know about the different types of masks, I hope that you can make an informed decision about how to protect yourself against COVID. Along with other proactive measures, masks can really play a huge part in protecting you if you use them properly. Wear them properly, wear the right ones, and wear them whenever you need to. As always, remember to be safe and proactive when it comes to your health!

CDC : Extra Tips

  • Make sure to wear your masks in public! Especially if you have a medical condition, masks are an important layer of self-protection against getting the virus.
  • Make sure your masks fit snugly and properly. Make sure there are no gaps where air can leak out, and that your nose is inside the mask.
  • Remember that you can adjust your mask within certain limits for comfort. If there are any problems with the fit of your mask, you can adapt and adjust. You can wear multiple layers, wear mask braces, or even tie the straps to find that perfect fit!
