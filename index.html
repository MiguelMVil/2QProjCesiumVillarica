What is COVID ProAct?

This is a website with helpful info on proactive ways to prevent the spread of COVID-19. This info is collected and compiled from many reliable sources such as the CDC and WHO. As opposed to measures such as treatment and isolation of people with COVID, these methods discussed in this website are things that you can do to fight COVID before even getting it. These measures include masks, social distancing, quarantines, and general hygiene practices.
